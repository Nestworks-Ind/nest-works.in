About NestWorks

Built from the field,
not from a whiteboard.

NestWorks builds focused desktop software for performance engineers — tools born from real enterprise engagements, not theoretical requirements. Every product exists because the problem was real, the manual effort was unacceptable, and no adequate solution existed.

The work behind NestWorks spans 11 years of performance engineering across 40+ enterprise clients in USA insurance, retirement, and financial services. That experience is encoded into every design decision.

The result is a growing suite of desktop products that eliminate the manual, repetitive work that no performance engineer should still be doing in 2026.

The NestWorks principle
Desktop-first. No cloud costs. No IT dependency. No data leaving your machine. Your workstation is more than powerful enough — the products just needed to exist. Each one targets a single problem and solves it completely, with deterministic output and zero compromise on accuracy.
