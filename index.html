About

I got tired of solving the same problems over and over again.

I'm Sreenu Gali — a performance engineer with over a decade of hands-on experience across enterprise insurance, retirement, and financial platforms. I've led performance engineering teams, managed 40+ client relationships, and built testing strategies at scale across some of the largest platforms in the USA.

11 years. 40+ clients. 15+ major releases a year. And in every single engagement, the same story — spending the majority of my time on work that should have been automated years ago. Correlation by hand. Assertions written one by one. Test data built in spreadsheets.

But through all of it — the products never caught up with the work. So I started building them myself.

Why I started NestWorks
NestWorks is not a company. It is my personal initiative — a growing library of desktop products built to eliminate the manual, repetitive work that no performance engineer should still be doing in 2026. Every product here started as something I built because I needed it and couldn't find it elsewhere.

Desktop-first. No cloud costs. No IT dependency. No data leaving your machine. Your workstation is more than powerful enough. The products just needed to exist.
