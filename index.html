How It Works

No servers. No cloud bills.
Just your machine.

NestWorks products are desktop applications — built to eliminate the need for cloud infrastructure entirely. Everything runs on your machine, so you pay nothing extra beyond the product itself.

01
⬇
Download the installer

Visit nest-works.in, pick your product, and download the installer for your operating system. One file. No account needed to download.

02
🖥
Install on your machine

Run the installer like any desktop application. No server to provision, no cloud account to set up, no IT team involved. Ready to use immediately — zero infrastructure cost.

03
▶

Launch the application from your desktop. Clean, focused interface — everything you need is right there. No manuals, no onboarding sessions required.

04

Get results instantly

The tool does the heavy lifting. Hours of manual work completed in minutes — and everything is processed entirely on your machine. NestWorks never reads, stores, or transmits your data. Your files stay yours, always.

Our Thinking
Why pay for cloud infrastructure
when your machine is powerful enough?

Performance testing products don't need expensive servers or cloud subscriptions to work well. Your laptop or workstation is more than capable of running the entire toolchain. NestWorks was built on this belief — desktop-first products that eliminate infrastructure costs without compromising on capability.

Whether you're a business owner or a performance engineer — powerful, focused software should be within reach. We build products that solve one problem exceptionally well, for the people who need them most.

🎯
One product, one problem
We don't build general-purpose platforms. Each product targets one specific pain point in the performance engineering workflow and solves it completely.
⚡
Built for real workflows
Every product is designed around how performance engineers actually work — JMeter-native, no forced methodology changes, no learning curve.
🖥
Desktop-first, always offline
No cloud infrastructure, no monthly server bills, no SaaS subscriptions. Every product runs entirely on your machine — the same hardware you already own. Maximum capability, minimum cost.

Built by a performance engineer

About
I got tired of solving the same
problems over and over again.

I'm Sreenu Gali — a performance engineer with over a decade of hands-on experience across enterprise insurance, retirement, and financial platforms. I've led performance engineering teams, managed client relationships, and built testing strategies that run at scale across some of the largest platforms in the USA. Deep experience in performance testing and engineering products.

11 years. 40+ clients. 15+ major releases a year. And in every single engagement, the same story — spending the majority of my time on work that should have been automated years ago. Correlation by hand. Assertions written one by one. Test data built in spreadsheets. Monitoring dashboards rebuilt from scratch for every project.

But through all of it — the products never caught up with the work. So I started building them myself.

Why I started NestWorks
NestWorks is not a company. It is my personal initiative — a growing library of desktop products built to eliminate the manual, repetitive work that no performance engineer should still be doing in 2026. Every product here started as something I built because I needed it and couldn't find it elsewhere.

The principle is simple: I should be spending my time analysing results, not preparing for them. And so should you.

Desktop-first. No cloud costs. No IT dependency. No data leaving your machine. Your workstation is more than powerful enough. The products just needed to exist.
